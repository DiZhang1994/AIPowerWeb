Meet our token PowerPoints

PPT is issued per renewable energy project. PPT can be purchased with Ethereum/US dollar. The amount that gathered will be used to fund the project itself. The holder of PPT of a project is therefore entitled to receive green energy service this project will be producing in the future.

Solar Power Plant Fundraising

Holder purchases PowerPoints with a money today at a discount price, and entitled for amount of green energy, or relevant service, that the project will produce in the future.
